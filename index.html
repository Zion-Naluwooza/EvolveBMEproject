About Us

Evolve started with a simple truth: Uganda isn't short on health innovators, but on platforms that truly support them. As biomedical problem solvers ourelves, we saw that too many powerful ideas die in silence. Brilliant startups were building real solutions, but lacked visibility, credibility, and proper channels needed for them to grow. So we built Evolve: A platform that spotlights African health startups, amplifies their impact and connects them to the people, partners, and opportunities they need to thrive. We know the work you're doing is hard. This platform is where your impact gets seen and amplified.

Mission

We spotlight and support startups solving Africa's most urgent healthcare challenges.

Vision

To ignite and grow Africa's boldest health innovations.

Core Values

Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, Excellence, and Commitment to Patient Care.

Company Overview

We are building the Ecosysystem layer that helps Ugandan health innovators stand out, collaborate and win.
